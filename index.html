About

I am a Toronto native who has an interest in multi-discplinary problems. I've spent the last few years co-founding a startup, Tandem Health, and working as a analytics and product management consultant. Outside of my profesional life, I've enjoyed my travels and studies across South-East Asia, as well as a brief stint in South America.

I'm always open to chatting about just about anything related to the future and technology. In my free time I like to play soccer, video games, and read historical accounts of war campaigns.

Work

Consulting

I am Management Consulting Analyst at Accenture (Applied Intelligence), the world's leading technology and digital tranformation consultancy. I work to help clients explore and navigate new operating and business models to become more customer-centric. My experience and passion lives between omni-channel marketing, applied analytics, and product management.

Programming

My development work portfolio consists of HTML and CSS projects demonstrating the fundamentals and latest best practices in web design, semantic html and accessibility. Going forward, I will extending my portfolio to include projects integrating Javascript, using popular front and back-end frameworks.

Entrepreneurship

I've co-founded a digital health platform, Tandem Health, as a comprehensive approach to therapy and mental well-being. Tandem Health acts as a companion application to patients in a cognitive behaviourial therapy (CBT) program, and as an enabler to evidence-based treatment for therapists and coaches. Our team has success piloting with academic institutions and clinics, with myself responsible of operations, finances and go-to-market strategy.
